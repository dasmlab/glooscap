5. Glooscap (Mi’kmaq & Wabanaki Confederacy)

Glooscap creates order, tames monstrous beings, and teaches humans their ways of living. He’s often described as a giver of knowledge, giver of the “first teachings,” including the organization of speech and storytelling.



7. Yakwil Gakw (Gitxsan) — The Speaker Role

Not a “god,” but the Gitxsan have ancient figures who embody the Speaker role, responsible for translating clan law into oral declarations. Many Gitxsan oral histories elevate these speakers to mythically-charged statuses.
